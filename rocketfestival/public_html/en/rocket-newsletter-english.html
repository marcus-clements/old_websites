Posted April 19th, 2008 by RayVaughn
in
  • Rocket Newsletter

New Bookings

The very exciting news today is that Flamenco legend Paco Moyano and his band will be sharing with us a beautiful and stirring traditional flamenco from the very roots of the genre. Paco is one of the greats from the traditional school and his songs tell of the harsh realities of life. Whether you can understand the words or not the passion in his voice and the haunting evocative strains of the guitar and subtle flamenco rhythms will leave only the most stony hearted without a tear in their eye. It's a very proud moment for Rocket to be able to provide a stage for Alhama de Granada's celebrated resident and we are sure you will enjoy the moment as much as we will.

Also booked this week for the main stage are local rockers 5 Duros, Granada's mestizo heroes Los Vecinos del Callejón (The neighbour from the lane) and Swedish psychedelic rock newcomers The Movements.
