Gridio Interactive Audio Visual InstallationBrightonart presents the Netsounds audiovisual production workshop. This event is in association with Same Sky and occurs in the frame of “THE GREAT ESCAPE” Festival - Brighton UK.
More information about the workshop on the Netsounds website

Brightonart will lead an audiovisual production workshop & networking event, focused around their Pompidou Centre commissioned interactive installation Gridio

Students and educators will be invited to join this workshop where participants will be helped through the process of creating their own Gridio audio-visual composition. At the end of the event the day's compositions will be loaded onto the actual Gridio installation, which will be installed specially for the event in the workshop space. Participants will then be able to experience their own interactive composition.
